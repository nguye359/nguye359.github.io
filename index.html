Battery Meter with Assembly

x64 little endian

The program was originally written in C. Then was converted to assembly to show that both version could still produce the same result. This forces a strong understanding at the low level of what actually happens in the registers. Also gives an overview of how manually written X64 assembly is coded compare to optimization techniques such as -01, -02, -03, and -0fast.

  • Date: October 2019
  • Language: Assembly
  • Category: Battery Simmulation
